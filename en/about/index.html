Hi, I’m Miao Hancheng

What this site covers

I work at Temu and I am also pursuing a Ph.D. in Computer Science at Arizona State University. This site is a long-form notebook for four recurring themes:

  • AI: LLM systems, NLP, evaluation, and applied engineering.
  • Quant: low-frequency quantitative research, backtesting, and tooling.
  • Career: decisions about research, industry, and long-term growth.
  • Immigration: process notes, timelines, and practical lessons.

Where to start

  • Start with AI if you want technical work.
  • Use Quant for trading research and experiments.
  • Use Career and Immigration for life and relocation notes.
  • Jump to Search if you already know the topic.

Research and engineering focus

  • Applied LLM and NLP systems.
  • Causal inference and machine learning methods.
  • Data infrastructure, PySpark, Hive SQL, and production engineering.

Open source

  • pysmatch: matching algorithms and causal inference tooling.
  • llm_stock_report: a stock monitoring bot combining LLM reasoning, Qlib-style features, and LightGBM signals.
